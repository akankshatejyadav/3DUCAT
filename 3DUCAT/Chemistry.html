Buck minister Fullurence

Buckminsterfullerene is a type of fullerene with the formula C60. It has a cage-like fused-ring structure (truncated icosahedron)
that resembles a soccer ball (football), made of twenty hexagons and twelve pentagons, with a carbon atom at each vertex of
each polygon and a bond along each polygon edge.
